  • Bamboo Bicycle

    Currently a work in progress, this project focuses on creating a bicycle combining bamboo - a natural material with a tradition in construction - with carbon fiber - a space age technology - a functional and beautiful art piece can be created.

    View Project
  • Senior Capstone Project in Engineering

    Worked in a team with Depuy Synthes Mitek Sports Medicine to expand their line of orthopedic arthroscopic surgical devices. Based on simulation, lab testing, and surgeon feedback, designs were developed and a proof-of-concept prototype was delivered.

    View Project
  • Sculptural Bicycle Design: Xantus

    Named after a species of hummingbird, this functional carbon fiber sculptural piece exists in the intersection of art and engineering, forcing the engineering feat of a bicycle into a sculptural form that plays on the traditional bicycle and the human body that powers it.

    View Project
  • Integrated Product Design: Sprout

    Working in a team of design, business, and engineering students, an aquaponics system was designed as an educational product for children that engaged the entire family in learning about water conservation, and Sprout was born.
