About Me

Meet Charlie:

I am currently in my final year at Franklin W. Olin College of Engineering in Needham, Massachusetts, studying Mechanical Engineering. I became an engineer because I have always been interested in designing, building, and making things. I find that engineering lets me explore the way that objects interact with one another and the world around them in the designs I create and explore.

I am an extremely passionate person; I often enjoy getting lost in interesting work and activities. I work hard and play harder. In my free time I enjoy goofing off with friends, kicking a ball, or riding a bike. I am always looking for a new project to occupy my time: I've made everything from armored gauntlets to copper jewelry to a Mobius gear. For a sample of my work, please read my portfolio.

In addition I love to talk with people and potential employers about my work, so please contact me.
